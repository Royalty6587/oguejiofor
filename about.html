About Us

About Us The Nwakaibeya Ogujiofor Foundation is established with a vision of compassion and commitment. The Foundation is a non-profit organization driven by the desire to make a positive impact in the lives of disadvantaged children, particularly those affected by hunger and malnutrition. We recognize hunger as a destabilizing force for families and a significant risk factor for children's health. We are focused on disabled and underprivileged children lacking essential resources. We reach out to them on the streets, schools, and homes to provide vital necessities like food, water, clothing, and shelter. Emphasizing collaborative efforts, we advocate for pulling resources together to effectively address the challenges of hunger and deprivation faced by these vulnerable children. Our goal is not only to alleviate immediate suffering but also to foster lasting change, ensuring a future of prosperity and opportunity for all. Our foundation is named in honor of Nwakaibeya Ogujiofor, a beloved community leader known for his dedication to helping those in need.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide comprehensive support and care for disabled and underprivileged children, empowering them to reach their full potential despite any challenges they may face. Through various programs and initiatives, we aim to enhance their quality of life, promote their rights, and foster their holistic development.

What We Do

Basic Needs Support

We recognize the importance of meeting basic needs such as food, shelter, and clothing. Our foundation provides essential support to families struggling to make ends meet, ensuring that children have access to nutritious meals, safe housing, and adequate clothing.

Medical Care and Rehabilitation:

We provide access to quality healthcare services and rehabilitation programs tailored to the unique needs of disabled children, ensuring they receive the treatment and support necessary for their well-being.

Education and Skill Development

Education is a fundamental right, and we are committed to ensuring that every child, regardless of their abilities or background, has access to quality education. We offer scholarships, tuition assistance, and vocational training programs to empower children with the knowledge and skills they need to build a brighter future.

Community Outreach and Advocacy

Community Outreach and Advocacy We actively engage with the community to raise awareness about the rights and needs of disabled and underprivileged children, advocating for their inclusion and equal opportunities. Through workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns. We strive to create a more compassionate and understanding society
